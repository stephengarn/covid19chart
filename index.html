Canada's COVID-19 situation as represented in a chart.

This chart dates back to when the pandemic began, to the current week ending every Saturday. As we can see the curve starting to flatten, this has been due to severe COVID-19 related restrictions on the lives of Canadians. This visual representation of the data is brought to you by a combination of myself, and the good people at about-corona.net and Chartjs. This project is far from perfect, but it gives you a decent idea of current situation.
